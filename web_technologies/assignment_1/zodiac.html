Personality of Zodiac Signs

Aries
Aries are known for being confident, enthusiastic, and energetic. They are natural leaders and enjoy taking charge of situations.
Taurus
Tauruses are known for being practical, reliable, and determined. They are hardworking and have a strong sense of financial security.
Gemini
Geminis are known for being curious, adaptable, and versatile. They enjoy learning new things and are skilled at communicating.
Cancer
Cancers are known for being sensitive, nurturing, and protective. They have a strong emotional connection to their family and home.
Leo
Leos are known for being charismatic, generous, and proud. They enjoy being in the spotlight and have a strong sense of self-importance.
